Lemon Garlic Chicken

Description

This recipes is created for beginner who've never touch a pan before.

Ingradients

  • garlic
  • lemon
  • chicken

Steps

  1. put the butter into the pan, wait until the butter boil
  2. friend the garlic with butter
  3. friend the chicken with low heat about 5 min per 1 side of chicken
  4. cut the lemon and put it on top of chicken
